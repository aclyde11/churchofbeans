Scene 1, Act 1: The Awakening

Cast of Characters

Soprano - Elisabetta: The protagonist, seeking truth
Tenor - Marco: The mysterious guide
Chorus: Voices of nature and memory

The stage is dimly lit. A single spotlight illuminates the center where ELISABETTA stands, dressed in flowing white robes. The CHORUS is positioned in shadows around the edges of the stage. Gentle orchestral music builds, suggesting the coming dawn.

ELISABETTA:

Within this darkness, I await the light,
My soul adrift on waves of memory.
What visions dance beyond my mortal sight?
What whispers call from realms I cannot see?

CHORUS:

(softly)
Awaken, seeker, from your slumber deep,
The veil grows thin between the worlds tonight.
What secrets do you wish the stars to keep?
What shadows do you banish with your light?

A second spotlight gradually illuminates MARCO, who enters from stage right. He moves with deliberate steps, carrying a lantern that casts flickering patterns across the stage.

MARCO:

I come bearing flames of ancient knowing,
To guide your steps through darkness unexplored.
Like rivers to the sea, forever flowing,
Our paths converge by destinies assured.

ELISABETTA:

Who sends you to my side at this strange hour?
What purpose draws you to my questioning?

MARCO:

Not who, but what—the ever-turning wheel,
The dance of stars, the rhythm of the spheres.
Tempus fugit, memento vivere—
Time flies, remember to live, while moments disappear.

The lights slowly brighten, suggesting dawn. The CHORUS moves forward, forming a semicircle around ELISABETTA and MARCO. The music swells with strings and woodwinds.

CHORUS:

The awakening begins, the journey starts,
As morning light dispels the shadowed night.
Two souls entwined by fate's mysterious arts,
Must seek the truth beyond what meets the sight.

ELISABETTA:

Then I shall follow where your lantern leads,
Through darkened paths and gardens unexplored.
Though caution whispers, courage intercedes—
What treasures wait beyond this threshold door?

MARCO:

Not treasures as the world has come to know,
But wisdom ancient as the mountain stone.
The seeds of truth we carefully must sow,
To reap the harvest from what has been grown.

MARCO extends his hand to ELISABETTA. After a moment's hesitation, she takes it. The lights shift to a warm golden hue as they move together toward stage left. The CHORUS follows behind them as the music builds to a crescendo.

ELISABETTA and MARCO:

(in unison)
Through shadow and light, through wisdom and wonder,
We journey together, no pathway asunder.
The quest has begun, the story unfolds,
As destiny's tapestry gently unrolls.

The lights slowly fade except for the lantern MARCO carries. As they exit stage left, the CHORUS remains, their voices diminishing to whispers.

CHORUS:

(voices fading)
The first step is taken, the journey's begun,
What ends in the evening must start with the sun.
Follow the lantern, seek what is true,
The answers you seek are already in you.

The stage darkens completely. A single note lingers in the air as the curtain falls on Act 1.
